Audience

There are three groups of people that are targeted concerning this site:

Residents

This site is meant to be helpful to residents by providing a resource with relevant, local information about other residents, nearby businesses, upcoming events, and more!

Businesses

Businesses will be able to network with residents, local government leaders, and other groups to organize events, raise funds, and provide other community support while at the same time providing a way to network with others.

Visitors

We love visitors here in Arco! Our aim is to provide interested individuals and groups a valuable resource of information of various sites and events, as well as the interesting historical timeline of our city.

Personas

A list of personas who might find our site helpful.

Local Business & Sons, Co.

This company has been around since the first plot of land broke ground in Arco, Idaho. Building homes and doing general construction for over 100 years, this family company has been passed down from father to son for over 3 generations.

Because this company has always been involved with the community, they greatly appreciate the resources to be found and used on this site:

  • View lists of upcoming community events
  • View the contact information for the local governmnent and other organizations
  • Be able to submit donations to various charitable organizations

The Smitherson Family

The Smitherson Family have lived in L.A. for over 1 generation. Papa and Mama Smitherson have four children: 2 boys and two girls, all under the age of ten.

The Smithersons have heard about the wonderful town of Arco, Idaho and have always wanted to visit. In preparation for their road trip over spring break, Papa and Mama google "Arco, Idaho" and find the chamber of commerce website, which supplied them with the following:

  • A historic timeline of the city
  • A list of sites to visit to get the full "Arco experience"
